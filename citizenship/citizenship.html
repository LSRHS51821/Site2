Citizenship

Honesty

Defintion: fairness and straightforwardness of conduct, adherence to the facts

Being honest is essential if one wants to be a good citizen because by being honest, you are not hiding anything of significance, you cannot deceive another and hurt them.

Compassion

Definition: sympathetic concern for the sufferings or misfortunes of others

It is necessary to have compassion because then you have the ability to care about others. You can pick someone up if they are down or help them through something hard.

Respect

Defintion: a feeling of deep admiration for someone or something elicited by their abilities, qualities, or achievements

By being respectful towards others and their belongings is crucial in order to maintain a sense of trust with those around you.

Responsibility

Definition: the state or fact of having a duty to deal with something or of having control over someone

Responsibility is key to being a good citizen so that people will take ownership of their duties or mistakes.

Courage

Definition: the ability to do something that frightens one, strength in the face of pain or grief
